Get started with SourceV1

Join our community. The SourceV1 community.

We're a community of North Sydney Boys students, lead by a small team of developers, to accelerate efficiency and effectivity of learning through the automation of content sharing at a scalable level.

  • 1

    Connect with like-minded teenagersWith an active Discord server, where you can ask for assistance for homework or academic related tasks within North Sydney Boys High School.

  • 2

    Unparalleled Development and verificationWe regularly commit to our main branch to maintain an updated repository that is capable of handling scalability and utilises discord API's latest features. Users are verified to ensure legitimacy and all content that you may be able to view must meet quality standards, ensuring high quality submissions.

  • 3

    Submit content and be rewardedAs you submit content, you receive a form of currency - tokens that allow you to access a channel filled with other user submitted content. You only receive these once your content passes our quality standards.
